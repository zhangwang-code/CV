Biography

I am a research assistant in spatial-temporal database Group, Department of Computer Science, South-Central University for Nationalities, working with Prof. Yanhong Li and Prof. Jun Qin. Befort that, I obtained my M.Phil in South-Central University for Nationalities at 2020 (GPA:3.56), supervised by Prof. Yanhong Li. Previously, I received B.S. degree from Department of Computer in Hubei University of Education at 2016 (GPA:3.34, Top-1/49).

I have broad research interests in spatial information and communication, and multimedia network technology. I have dedicated to address pub/sub problem with Boolean expression in the Internet of Industrial Vehicles, and I also have tried to answer Why-not questions on augmented spatial keyword Top-k queries and direction-aware augmented spatial keyword Top-k queries. Recently I am focusing on answering Why-not questions on top-k queries over moving objects. In the following few years, I would like to answer Why-not questions on other types of queries such as graph queries and trajectory queries. And I will focus on solving the challenge of how to adjust the index structure automatically when spatial objects are in motion.
