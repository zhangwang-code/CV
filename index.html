Biography

I am a research assistant in spatial-temporal database Group, Department of Computer Science, South-Central University for Nationalities, working with Prof. Yanhong Li and Prof. Jun Qin. Befort that, I obtained my M.Phil in South-Central University for Nationalities at 2020 (GPA:3.56/4, Top-3/16), supervised by Prof. Yanhong Li. Previously, I received B.S. degree from Department of Computer in Hubei University of Education at 2016 (GPA:3.34/4, Top-1/49), supervised by A/Prof. Yuda Shi.

I have broad research interests in spatial-temporal data management, graph data analysis and the embedded system. I have dedicated to address pub/sub problem with Boolean expression in the Internet of Industrial Vehicles, and I also have tried to answer Why-not questions on augmented spatial keyword Top-k queries, direction-aware augmented spatial keyword Top-k queries and spatial keyword Top-k queries over moving objects. Recently I am focusing on answering privacy protection-based Why-not questions and geo-social data preprocessing. In the following few years, I would like to study in processing spatial-social data and trajectory data with AI.
